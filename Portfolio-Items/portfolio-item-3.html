Sword Model

The M9 Beretta is a well-known and popular firearm, and I wanted to create a high-quality 3D model of the iconic weapon. Using Maya, I modeled and UV wrapped the gun, paying close attention to small details and ensuring a clean and efficient topology.

Next, I moved on to texturing the model in Substance Painter, applying realistic materials and adding subtle wear and tear to create a more authentic look. Finally, I rendered the gun using Marmoset Toolbag, utilizing its advanced lighting and shading features to create a stunningly realistic and visually appealing final product.

The end result is a highly-detailed and accurate 3D model of the M9 Beretta that is optimized for use in games. This model can be purchased on my Gumroad.
