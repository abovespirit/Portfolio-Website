Grace Residential

While still in high school, I had the opportunity to work on a collaborative paid project with a group of fellow classmates. Our task was to create a preview of a kitchen and house layout for one of their properties using 3D rendering software. It was an exciting project, and we knew that we had to work together closely to deliver a high-quality result.

Through effective communication and collaboration, we were able to create multiple renders that accurately portrayed the proposed design. The project allowed us to apply our individual strengths and skills to create a cohesive final product, and we were thrilled to have exceeded our client's expectations. This project taught me the value of teamwork and the importance of communication when working on complex projects.
