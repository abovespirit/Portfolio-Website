Valorant Vandal

This was the second project for my Modeling 2 class, which was also my first venture into hard surface weapon modeling. The project involved recreating the Vandal, a popular weapon from Riot Games' Valorant. Looking back, I'm pleased with how the modeling turned out, but I recognize that there is always room for improvement, and I plan to revisit the model in the future to refine and redo elements such as the texure work and UV mapping.

Throughout the project, I leveraged industry-standard tools, such as Maya, Substance Painter, and Marmoset Toolbag, to create an end result that I was pleased with. Most importantly working on this project piqued my interest in hard surface weapon modeling, and gave me ideas for future projects.
