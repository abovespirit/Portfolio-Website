Custom M4 (Ongoing Project)

Another ongoing side project of mine that stemmed as a result of an independent study during my classes at UCF. During my independent study, I followed along with Tim Bergholz's Game-Ready Revolver Tutorial. I wanted to apply this knowledge that I learned to my own weapon model. This is my current result of following Tim's pipeline which utilizes Blender's remesh workflow and several UV tools.

I still wan to adjust many of the roughness values to make the gloss look more realistic.

The lazer attachment still needs lots of love...

Yup, no Zbrush needed, Blender was all I needed for this highpoly!
