Contemporary LA Bedroom

One of my earlier projects from high school involved using 3ds Max and Vray to create realistic renders. I was responsible for modeling various objects and placing them strategically to achieve a cohesive scene. Through this project, I honed my skills in 3D modeling and gained a deeper understanding of lighting and composition in digital art. This experience was formative for me as a young artist and continues to inspire me in my current work.
