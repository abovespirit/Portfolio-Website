VR Penthouse Enviroment

Inspired by my interest in architectural visualization and my experience in exploring the capabilities of virtual reality during my internship, I decided to create an apartment that I could explore in VR. Using Maya, I modeled many of the assets and gained valuable experience using Unity to bring everything together. In my research of creating environments for virtual reality, I discovered VRChat, a Unity-based game that allows creators to upload and explore their own virtual worlds.

Through this project, I learned the importance of optimizing my assets by compressing textures and baking lighting, as well as utilizing reflection probes and shaders with masks to create a realistic look and feel. This project was a challenging yet rewarding experience that further ignited my interest in VR, and creating environments.

This project can be viewed publicly in VR by downloading the game on Steam.
