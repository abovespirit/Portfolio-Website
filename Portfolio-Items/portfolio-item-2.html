Nike Pegasus 86

I wanted to challenge myself by exploring footwear design, even though I had never attempted it before. I chose the Nike Pegasus 86 for my inspiration. I decided to take an unconventional approach by creating a low poly blockout in Maya instead of creating it solely in Zbrush. This allowed me to focus on the basic shape and structure of the shoe design without getting bogged down in the details.

I then imported the blockout into Zbrush to refine and add intricate details before baking it back down onto the original low poly model. Finally, I used Substance Painter to texture the model and add color, pattern, and materials. This was the end result, and while unconventional, it allowed me to experiment with different tools and techniques to achieve my vision. Although it was my first attempt at footwear design, I am proud of the result and excited to explore this area further.
