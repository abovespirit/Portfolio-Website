VR Bar Enviroment

Shortly after completing my first Unity scene for VR, I was commissioned to create a bar scene for someone to use within virtual reality. This project was a great opportunity for me to apply and further solidify what I had learned from my previous experience with Unity. I delved deeper into the possibilities of aesthetics within the engine and used my skills in modeling, texturing, and lighting to create an immersive and visually stunning environment.

One of the highlights of this project was the full-screen display I created, which can play visuals with two side angles of the stage. To give the screen a more realistic look, I used a shader that imitates individual RGB dots. Overall, this commission was a challenging and rewarding experience that allowed me to hone my skills and take my VR creations to the next level.

This project can be viewed publicly in VRChat by downloading the game on Steam.
