Arthrex operating room

During my internship with the medical company Arthrex, I had the opportunity to work alongside their animation team and explore the exciting world of VR/AR technologies. As part of my internship, my main project involved recreating an operating room using 3D modeling software. My goal was to make it as accurate and realistic as possible, matching the layout and equipment of the real-life operating room available on campus.

Through this project, I gained valuable experience in 3D modeling and visualization, as well as a deeper understanding of the medical industry and the importance of accuracy in medical simulations. Working with Arthrex was an incredibly rewarding experience, and I am grateful for the skills and knowledge I gained during my time there.
