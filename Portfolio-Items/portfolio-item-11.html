Messy Apartment

TOne of my earliest projects that I wanted to showcase is a 3D modeling and environment design project that I completed during one of my classes. Throughout the semester, I dedicated myself to modeling various objects to populate a scene. Although the texturing work was not up to par, I was able to improve my modeling skills significantly, and it was also my first attempt at environment design. This project was an essential milestone for me, as it helped me realize my passion for 3D modeling and pushed me to continue exploring and perfecting my skills in the field. As with many of my early projects, I created this with 3ds max.
